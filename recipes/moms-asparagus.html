Mom's Asparagus

This is my Mom's asparagus recipe. It tastes incredible, and it also makes multiple orifices stink.

Ingredients

  • Asparagus
  • Garlic, a fuck ton
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Break hard stems off asparagus
  2. Mince the fuck ton of garlic
  3. Cover the asparagus in a hefty portion of olive oil
  4. Cover oiled asparagus in salt, pepper, and the minced garlic
  5. Roast at 400 until asparagus is is fork tender
  6. Eat it.
