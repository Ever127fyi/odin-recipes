Shrimp Alfredo

This is shrimp alfredo. It is delicious, and it is also delicious. It pairs well with any kind of wine. If you like wine.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Parmesan
  • Pasta of your choice.
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Cream if you're weak.

Steps

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Cook your shrimp while the water boils.
  3. Strain your pasta, but save some water.
  4. In a separate pan create your alfredo sauce with the cheese and butter, add water a little at a time.
  5. place pasta in sauce, and add shrimp.
  6. Enjoy!
